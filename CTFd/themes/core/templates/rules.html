Read Instructions Carefully


Rules

1. Do not attack the backend infrastructure of the CTF.
2. Do not attack other teams playing in the CTF.
3. Do not brute-force the flag submission form.
4. Do not exchange flags or write-ups/hints of the challenges with other teams.
5. Do not violate Hackerearth's Terms of Service. You can read it here.
6. Do not try to DDoS the challenges or make actions that could lead to this result. For example, brute force or use of automated tools with many threads.
7. Do not be part of more than one team within the same CTF.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on this website unless otherwise explicitly stated as allowed in the mission statement:

1. Using automated scanners
2. Using brute force attacks
3. Denial of Service attacks
4. Attacking other student machines in challenges where you might achieve a shell on the vulnerable system
5. Attacking the lab infrastructure
6. Users violating the above will be either temporarily or permanently banned from the website.
